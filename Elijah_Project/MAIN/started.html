Getting Started

The MRSL is open to players of all levels who want to play soccer in a relaxed, friendly environment. Even if you've never played soccer before, the MRSL is a great place to start.

To get a feel for our league, we recommend you call us at the number below or stop by the fields to talk to one or our coordinators and get the details on our next all-team practice/workshop day.

If you're interested in joining up, you'll need to complete some paperwork and pay a membership fee:

  • Child/Teen (6-18): $50
  • Student (19-24): $50
  • Adult (25-59): $65
  • Senior (60+): $40

We look forward to seeing you on the field!
